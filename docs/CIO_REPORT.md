## 1. Executive Summary

We are proposing the deployment of the **Project Intake Agent** as our organization's first strategic "win" in AI adoption.

This is not an R&D experiment. It is a practical, operational tool designed to solve a specific business bottleneck: **Speed to Market.** By automating the initial discovery phase of new projects, we can significantly reduce the "fuzzy front end" delay, allowing our teams to move from *idea* to *execution* faster.

More importantly, this project establishes the **Governance & Architecture Blueprint** for how our Credit Union will safely build and deploy AI tools in the future.

---

## 2. Strategic Value: Why This? Why Now?

### A. Speed to Market (Operational Efficiency)
Currently, valuable IT resources are tied up in repetitive "discovery meetings" just to gather basic requirements.
*   **The Solution:** An always-on AI agent that conducts these interviews instantly.
*   **The Impact:** Projects arrive at the PMO/IT desk with a **Standardized PDF Brief** and **Risk Analysis** already complete. We skip the first 2-3 meetings and start working immediately.

### B. The "First Win" for AI Transformation
We have a roadmap for AI, but we need a tangible success story.
*   **Low Risk:** This system is internal-facing (staff only), minimizing reputational risk.
*   **High Visibility:** Every department (Marketing, Lending, HR) that requests a project will interact with it, demonstrating IT's innovation leadership.

---

## 3. The Blueprint for Safe AI

This project proves that we can innovate without compromising security. We have built a reusable architecture that addresses the Board's primary concerns about AI:

1.  **Data Sovereignty:** We use a **Hybrid Guardrail System** (Regex + NLP) to strip Member PII *before* it ever touches the AI model.
2.  **Deterministic Control:** The AI is "caged" within a strict State Machine. It follows our process; it does not improvise.
3.  **Auditability:** Every interaction is logged, and every output is reviewed by a human (via the generated report).

**This is the model we will use for future AI initiatives.**

---

## 4. Architecture & Scalability

We have adopted a **Modular, Cloud-Native** approach:
*   **Containerized (Docker):** Ready for Azure/AWS or On-Prem hosting.
*   **Integration-Ready:** Already connected to our PM tools (Monday.com/Jira).
*   **Cost-Effective:** Uses a "Model Mixing" strategy (GPT-4o for complex tasks, smaller models for simple ones) to optimize opex.
